About Me

My name is Aleksa Savic, I am from Serbia, and I am years old. I have been working with Frontend Web Development for a long time, and I enjoy creating projects and responsive websites.

I am persistent in everything I do and love to improve and learn more about web development. Most of my knowledge has been self-taught by following various tutorials, researching online, and taking courses like JavaScript and React Development.

I hope that one day I will have the opportunity to showcase my skills and collaborate with others in this field. My goal is to become a Full Stack Developer.

One aspect of web development that particularly fascinates me and that I enjoy is creating
ANIMATIONS.

I believe that animations not only enhance the visual appeal of a website but also improve the user experience, making it more interactive and dynamic.

Through animations, I can add "life" to static elements and create fluid, intuitive navigation that helps users better understand how to interact with the site.
